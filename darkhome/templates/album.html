Latest Album

Detailed information about the latest album:

Echoes in the Dark's latest album is a collection of dark, moody, and haunting songs that takes listeners on a journey through the shadows. The album features the band's signature sound, blending classic gothic rock with elements of darkwave and industrial, and features soaring vocals, driving beats, and haunting melodies.

The album was recorded over the course of several months in a dark and eerie studio deep in the heart of the gothic countryside, and was produced by the band's own Dante. With its powerful songs and evocative production, the latest album from Echoes in the Dark is a must-have for fans of gothic music.
